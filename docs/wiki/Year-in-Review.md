# Year in Review

The Year in Review is a full-year summary of your habit data — available any time from the Analytics screen, not just on January 1st.

---

## How to Open It

1. Tap **Analytics** in the bottom navigation bar
2. Tap the **Year in Review** banner near the top of the screen
3. Select a year (current year or previous year)

---

## What's Shown

| Stat | What it measures |
|------|-----------------|
| ✅ Habits Completed | Total individual habit completions in the year |
| 📅 Active Days | Days where you completed at least one habit, out of total days in the period |
| ⭐ Perfect Days | Days where every assigned habit was completed |
| 🔥 Best Streak | Longest consecutive active-day streak achieved in the year |
| 💪 Best Life Area | Your highest-completion life area, with percentage |
| ⚡ XP Earned | Estimated XP earned in the year (computed from daily entries) |

Your **current level** is shown separately below the stats grid.

---

## The Personal Headline

At the top of the screen, above the stats, is a single sentence generated from your data. It's calibrated to your consistency level — honest about where you stood, without judgment.

Examples:
- *"[Name] showed up 312 days out of 365 in 2025. That kind of consistency doesn't happen by accident. That's who you are now."*
- *"In 2025, [Name] was active 187 days. Longest streak: 34 days. The commitment is real. The next step is protecting more of your days."*

---

## Year Selection

The Year in Review shows two years: the current year and the previous year.

- In **January–March**, the previous year is shown first (you just finished it).
- In **April–December**, the current year is shown first.

Tap either chip to switch years. The screen reloads with data for the selected year.

---

## Sharing

Tap the **Share** button (or the share icon in the top bar) to send your Year in Review as plain text via any app — Messages, Notes, email, etc.

The shared text includes all stats and the personal headline, formatted for readability.

---

## Data Notes

- The Year in Review uses your current assigned habits as the reference set. Habits added or removed mid-year may cause slight variance in "total possible" calculations — this is expected.
- XP shown is an estimate computed from entries, not pulled from a historical ledger. It reflects how much you would have earned if check-ins were recorded daily.
- For the current year, the period ends at today's date rather than December 31st.
