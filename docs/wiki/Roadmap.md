## Planned

### Quick-Complete from Notification
Habit reminder notifications gain a **"Done"** action button. Tapping it marks the habit complete for today without opening the app.

### Habit Template Library
A curated library of pre-configured habit templates organized by archetype (Builder, Athlete, Student, Mindful). Templates pre-fill name, type, target, and reminder time — starting points, not prescriptions.

### Proactive Weekly Insight Notification
Every Sunday evening, one notification summarizing the week's most actionable insight — drawn from existing analytics data. One notification, honest, actionable.

### Adaptive Reminder Timing
After 14 days of data, the app analyzes what time you actually complete each habit and suggests moving the reminder to match. Also adds a streak-at-risk nudge if habits are unfinished near bedtime.

### Health Connect Integration
Auto-fill compatible habits (steps, sleep, active minutes) from Android Health Connect data. Shown as a suggestion during daily check-in — the user confirms or edits. No data leaves the device.

### Mood × Habit Correlation
A simple correlation in the Report screen: on days where all habits are completed, what is the average mood score vs. partial or zero-completion days? One sentence + a bar comparison using existing data.

## Philosophy

Every feature added must reflect the app's principle: **free, helpful, built for productive people**.

No engagement tricks. No gamification inflation. No dark patterns. Only genuine utility.
