# Gamification — XP, Levels & Badges

HabitPower's gamification is designed to reflect genuine effort — not manufactured drama or engagement tricks.

---

## XP (Experience Points)

You earn XP each day you check in habits:

| Action | XP |
|--------|----|
| Showing up (at least one habit done) | +12 |
| Each additional habit beyond the first | +2 (capped at +4) |
| Perfect day (all habits done) | +8 |
| Streak bonus | +6 per streak day, capped at +90 |

The streak bonus is the most significant lever — consistency compounds.

## Design Philosophy

The XP values are intentionally modest. A dedicated user with 5 habits and a consistent streak earns roughly:
- ~32 XP/day → Level 20 in ~8 months
- Level 30 in ~2 years  
- Level 50 in ~5–6 years

This reflects actual multi-year commitment — not inflated numbers that make people feel good in the short term and hollow in the long term. Every number here is meant to be honest.
