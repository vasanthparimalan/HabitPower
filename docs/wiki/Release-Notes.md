## v1.2 — Data Safety · Habit Lifecycle · Unlimited Progression · Year in Review
**Released:** 2026-04-15

### What's New

#### Data Safety
- **Android Auto Backup enabled.** Your habit history is now protected by your Google One backup schedule. A factory reset or new phone no longer means losing years of data. See [Your Data & Backup](Data-and-Backup) for how to verify your backup is running.
- **Export your data.** Admin › Export Data lets you save a CSV (for spreadsheet analysis) or a full JSON backup to any location you choose. No permissions required — you pick the destination with the system file browser.

#### Habit Lifecycle States
Habits now move through a lifecycle instead of piling up forever. Change a habit's status from **Admin › Habits** using the dropdown on any habit card.

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Active | Tracked daily (default) |
| Paused | Streak paused; reminders silenced. For travel, illness, or life events. |
| Retired | Discontinued. History preserved. Excluded from daily tracking. |
| Graduated | Automatic now. Moves to the **Identity Wall** on your Dashboard. |

The Identity Wall is a collapsible section on the Dashboard showing every habit you've internalized — a record of who you've become, not a graveyard of old goals.

#### Unlimited Level Progression
Level 20 is now a milestone, not a ceiling.
- Every level beyond 20 requires **8,000 more XP** than the previous level.
- Levels 21–50 have distinct titles: Philosopher, Virtuoso, Pathfinder … Undying.
- Level 51 and beyond shows "Level N" — the progression never ends.
- Four new mastery milestone badges:

| Level | Badge | Title |
|-------|-------|-------|
| 25 | 💠 | Mastermind |
| 30 | 🌌 | Eternal |
| 40 | ⚜️ | Mythic |
| 50 | 🏵️ | Undying |

#### Year in Review
A full-year summary screen accessible from **Analytics › Year in Review**.

- **Stats shown:** habits completed, active days, perfect days, best streak, best life area (by completion %), estimated XP gained.
- **Personal headline:** a single sentence generated from your consistency data.
- **Year selection:** covers both the current year and previous year — useful any time, not just January 1st.
- **Share:** tap the share button to send your Year in Review as plain text via any app.

### Fixes & Improvements
- Habit reminders no longer fire for Paused, Retired, or Graduated habits.
- XP display no longer shows `(MAX)` at Level 20.
