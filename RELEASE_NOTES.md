## v1.2 — Data Safety · Habit Lifecycle · Unlimited Progression · Year in Review
**Released:** 2026-04-15  
**Build:** versionCode 3

### What's New

#### Data Safety
- **Android Auto Backup enabled** — your habit history is now protected by Google One. A factory reset or new phone no longer means losing years of data.
- **Export your data** — Admin › Export Data lets you save a CSV (for spreadsheet analysis) or a full JSON backup to any location you choose. No cloud account required; you pick the folder.

#### Habit Lifecycle States
Habits now have a lifecycle — they grow with you instead of piling up forever.
- **Active** — tracked daily (default)
- **Paused** — streak paused, reminders silenced. Use for travel, illness, or life events.
- **Retired** — discontinued with history preserved. Excluded from daily tracking.
- **Graduated** — the habit is now automatic behavior. Moves to the **Identity Wall** on your Dashboard — a quiet record of who you've become.

Change a habit's status from Admin › Habits using the dropdown on any habit card.

#### Unlimited Level Progression
Level 20 ("Enlightened") is no longer the ceiling — it's the first milestone.
- Every level beyond 20 requires 8,000 more XP than the last.
- Levels 21–50 have distinct titles (Philosopher → Undying). Level 51+ shows "Level N".
- Four new mastery badges unlock at levels 25 (Mastermind), 30 (Eternal), 40 (Mythic), and 50 (Undying).

#### Year in Review
A full-year summary screen, accessible from Analytics.
- Shows: habits completed, active days, perfect days, best streak, best life area, estimated XP.
- Generates a personal headline from your consistency data.
- Shareable as plain text via your system share sheet.
- Covers current year and previous year — useful all year, not just on January 1st.

### Bug Fixes & Improvements
- Habit reminders no longer fire for Paused, Retired, or Graduated habits.
- Identity Wall on Dashboard hidden until at least one habit is graduated.
- XP display no longer shows "(MAX)" at Level 20.
